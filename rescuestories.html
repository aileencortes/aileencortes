Peanuts Story

Peanut was adopted on March 24th, 2022. She had spent almost 8 months in the shelter waiting on her forever home, until one day someone special came to visit her and fell in love with her and her personality. Now instead of being in a kennel she spends her surrounded by laughter and affection, long walks in the park, barking at the mail man, and stealing jars of Peanut Butter.
